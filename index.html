WEBSITES

A website is a collection of webpages that can be used to explain a show what your business does. It is an easy and effeicient way of marketing. Websites are devoloped using the skills acquired from web development.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Web development is the act of building a website using available resources like HTML for giving a skeletal look, CSS for designs and styling (Beautification) and a programming language for adding life and activity to a website.

Why choose to have a website for your company

  1. A company website can be accessed online, therefore easing the marketing of the company over boundaries.
  2. A company website can be used for communication within company employees, therefore making company day-to-day work maaged easily.
  3. A company website is a source of trust to customers as people are sure of the validity of the company as its website is allowed to run in a particular country.
  4. A company website increases sales of a company as people can easily access the company products, order for goods online and make payments, therefore increasing profits.
